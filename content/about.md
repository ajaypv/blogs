## About OpenDots

OpenDots is a social platform connecting users through shared interests, housing needs, and community spaces.

### Our Mission

To make local connections easier and more meaningful. Whether you're looking for a roommate, planning a group trip, or just want to find people who share your interests, OpenDots is here to help.

### The Team

We're a small team of developers and designers passionate about building tools that bring people together.
